The purpose of this webage is to act as a place to collect all your favourite streaming channels in one place.

You can click through them like you would the channels on your tv.

Click one of the buttons in the menu on the left to watch one of the default streams.

To add a stream, copy an iframe or video html element to the input field.

You can name your stream anything you'd like as long as it's 15 characters or less.
